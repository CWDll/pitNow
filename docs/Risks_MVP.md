# Risks & Controls

## Legal

- Classified as space rental, not repair service
- Legal allowlist enforced for self-maintenance tasks
- User self-repair agreement (only selected tasks)
- Consent evidence retained (checkbox/signature)

## Safety

- Mandatory agreement
- Photo logging
- Insurance in future phase
- Out-of-scope work prevention by task declaration + policy notice

## Operational

- DB enforced time conflict
- Automatic settlement
- Blocking includes end_time + 1 hour buffer
- Helper verification surcharge calculated transparently

## Partner Risk

- Clear liability separation
- Optional helper verification is post-work check, not repair execution
