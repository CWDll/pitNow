# Policies

## Reservation

- Minimum 1 hour
- 30 min extension unit
- Prepaid only

## No-show

- 10 min late → auto cancel (Phase2)

## Extra Fee

- Calculated per 30min
- Rounded up

## Check-in

- 4 vehicle photos required
- Without photo, timer cannot start

## Checkout

- Cleaning required
- Tool check required
- Extra fee auto calculated
